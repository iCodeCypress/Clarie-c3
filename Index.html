What is Construct 3?

Construct 3 is a game engine where you can create your own games. Then, they can add custom logic through events to make the game function properly. They can add their own custom characters and build their own maps in the game.

Who made Construct 3 when was it made?

The game engine Construct 3 was released on December 4, 2017 and was developed by the company Scirra Ltd.

What is an example of a popular game made by Construct 3?

A popular game made in Construct 3 is a called "Catmaze." Another example would be "Tag."

What was one of the game you made in Construct 3? What other game would you like to make?

One of the games that I made in construct 3 was a tower-defense game. A game i look forward to making in the future is a fantasy RPG game.
